Quick Instructions

  1. Have your letter-writers upload their letters at https://margymail.com/upload.
  2. Wait for emails from MARGY containing your letters’ mailto codes.
  3. Go to https://margymail.com/deliver and enter your mailto codes and the email addresses to which you want them sent, or deliver a single letter by sending an email to mailto_code@margymail.com with the relevant addresses in the email body.

  4. Wait for an automated email from MARGY, confirming which email addresses your letters have been sent to.

We also recommend adding your email address to MARGY’s email blast list, which we use to notify users of new features, outages, etc. You can add (or remove) your email address here.
