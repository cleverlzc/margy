Quick Instructions

  1. Have your letter-writers upload their letters through Upload a Letter.
  2. Wait for emails from MARGY containing your letters’ mailto codes.
  3. Make sure the addresses you want your letters sent to are on MARGY’s Whitelist.
  4. Go to Deliver Letters and enter your mailto codes and the email addresses to which you want those letters sent.

  5. Wait for a confirmation email from MARGY.

We also recommend adding your email address to MARGY’s email blast list, which we use to notify users of new features, outages, etc. You can add (or remove) your email address here.
