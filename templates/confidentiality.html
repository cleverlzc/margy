MARGY maintains confidentiality in two ways:

  1. MARGY encrypts all letters as they are uploaded, so that not even MARGY’s administrators can access them. A decryption key is generated and included as part of the mailto code sent to the applicant. That key is not saved anywhere else.

  2. MARGY only sends letters of recommendation to email addresses on its whitelist, all of which have been verified as belonging to a hiring entity. The whitelist appears below in its entirety, for purposes of transparency.

Want to see how MARGY does things for yourself? Check out the source code.

To request that an address be added to the whitelist, go to https://margymail.com/addrequest or send an email to admin@margymail.com, Subject: Whitelist Addition, with that email address and a link to official job ad that asks that letters be sent to that address.
