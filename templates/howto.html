How To...

Upload a Letter

Just go to http://margymail.com/upload or click the Upload button above. You will be presented with a form asking you to input your name, the applicant’s name (whom you wrote the letter for), and the applicant’s email address, along with the letter as a PDF.

Add an Address to the Whitelist

To request that an email (or emails) be added to the whitelist, send a message to admin@margymail.com, Subject: Whitelist Addition, with that email address and a link to an official job ad which asks that letters be sent to that email address.

During beta testing, you can add your email address to the whitelist automatically by sending a blank email to whitelist@margymail.com.
Part of the function of beta testing is to find out what sorts of things should go here. If you have a suggestion, please send it to admin@margymail.com. {% endblock %}
