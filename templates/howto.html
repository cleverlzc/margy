How To...

Upload a Letter

Go to https://margymail.com/upload or click the Upload a Letter button above. You will be presented with a form asking you to input your name, the applicant’s name (whom you wrote the letter for), and the applicant’s email address, along with the letter as a PDF.

Add an Address to the Whitelist

To request that an email (or emails) be added to the whitelist, send a message to admin@margymail.com, Subject: Whitelist Addition, with that email address and a link to an official job ad which asks that letters be sent to that email address.
